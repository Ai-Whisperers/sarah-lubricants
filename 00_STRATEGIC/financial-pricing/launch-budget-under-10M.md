## Revenue projection (Option B, 2 SKUs)

### Conservative scenario (the realistic one)

- **Catalog:** 2 SKUs (Bitchie 20ml, XXX For Her 15ml)
- **Retail prices (Gs):** 95,000 each (150% margin on landed)
- **Landed cost per unit (Gs):** ~5,500 (Bitchie) and ~5,000 (XXX)
- **Margin per unit (Gs):** ~89,500 and ~90,000
- **Monthly sales target:** 30 units total = ~Gs 2.85M revenue
- **Monthly profit (after variable costs):** ~Gs 2.7M

**Break-even:** first month (small fixed costs)

### Stretch scenario

- 60 units/month = ~Gs 5.7M revenue
- 100 units/month = ~Gs 9.5M revenue (Q4.5: Gs 5M target hit by month 4–5)
