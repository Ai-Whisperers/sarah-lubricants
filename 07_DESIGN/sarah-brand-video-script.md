# Sarah Brand Video Script — 60-90 Second Intro

**Date:** June 26, 2026
**Use:** IG bio, TikTok intro, B2B wholesale pitches, landing pages
**Format:** Vertical video (9:16), 60-90 seconds
**Tone:** Personal, warm, real (not polished/corporate)

---

## Goal of the video

Build trust in 60 seconds. Make viewer think: "This is a real person. She's been through what I'm going through. I trust her."

NOT: "She has a fancy production setup." Real > polished.

---

## Pre-production checklist

### Equipment (minimal)

- [ ] Smartphone (decent camera — iPhone 12+ / Samsung S21+)
- [ ] Tripod (Gs 30K)
- [ ] Lavalier mic (Gs 60K) — **CRITICAL for outdoor/noisy environments**
- [ ] Natural light (window, 9-11 AM or 3-5 PM)
- [ ] Plain background (white wall, cream curtain, plant in background)

### Setup

- [ ] Camera at eye level
- [ ] Distance: arm's length
- [ ] Frame: head + shoulders + some space above
- [ ] Lighting: face the window (light on your face)
- [ ] Quiet environment (no TV, no music)

### What to wear

- [ ] Simple, clean shirt (not branded)
- [ ] No logos
- [ ] No excessive jewelry
- [ ] Hair down or pulled back, your choice
- [ ] Comfortable, "this is me" feel

### What to have on hand

- [ ] 1 Sexitive product (Bitchie or Wet, original packaging)
- [ ] 1 candle or 1 oil (visual prop)
- [ ] Nothing else (keep simple)

## Script — 60-90 seconds (Spanish)

### Beat 1 — Hook (0-5 sec)

**[Camera tight on face. Confident, warm expression.]**

> "Soy Sarah. Y hace 6 meses descubrí algo que me voló la cabeza."

### Beat 2 — The discovery (5-20 sec)

**[Camera slightly wider. Show product in hand.]**

> "En Buenos Aires, en la fábrica de Sexitive, vi cómo se hacen los productos íntimos que usamos. Y me pregunté: ¿por qué en Paraguay no tenemos acceso a esto?"

### Beat 3 — The decision (20-40 sec)

**[Camera back on face. Personal, direct.]**

> "Entonces traje los primeros. No sabía si iba a funcionar. Pero las respuestas que recibí... me confirmaron que faltaba esto en Paraguay. Productos certificados. Empaque discreto. Atención personal."

### Beat 4 — The promise (40-65 sec)

**[Show product + candle prop. Calm, confident.]**

> "Hoy Enki Store es la primera distribuidora oficial de Sexitive Argentina en Paraguay. Cada producto viene con registro DINAVISA. Cada pedido llega en empaque genérico, sin logos. Y cada consulta la respondo yo, personalmente, por WhatsApp."

### Beat 5 — The CTA (65-80 sec)

**[Direct to camera. Smile.]**

> "Si querés probar algo nuevo, o necesitás reabastecerte, escribime. Catálogo completo en el link de mi bio."

**[End with: name on screen, WA link]**

> "Sarah Roig — Enki Store"

### Total runtime: 80 seconds

## Filming tips

### Do

- ✅ **Smile at the start** (warm, not corporate)
- ✅ **Look at the camera** (not at the screen)
- ✅ **Speak naturally** — like talking to a friend
- ✅ **Pause between sentences** (don't rush)
- ✅ **Show the product** (hold it, gesture to it)
- ✅ **Be specific** — "Hace 6 meses" not "recientemente"
- ✅ **End with action** — "escribime," "link en bio"

### Don't

- ❌ Don't read from a script (use bullet points + practice 3 times)
- ❌ Don't apologize for the video quality ("sorry, this is my first time")
- ❌ Don't be salesy ("¡Compra ya!")
- ❌ Don't use jargon (DINAVISA = say "registrados oficialmente")
- ❌ Don't make it too long (60-90 sec max)
- ❌ Don't be perfect — authenticity > production value
- ❌ Don't say "intimate" or "sex" too much (use "wellness," "intimate care," "personal care")

## B-roll suggestions (cutaways)

While you talk, cut to:
- 1 sec: B-roll of you unpacking a Sexitive product
- 1 sec: Close-up of the product label
- 1 sec: Close-up of discrete brown box (the packaging)
- 1 sec: Hand writing a thank-you note
- 1 sec: Your hand holding the candle/lubricante

Then cut back to face.

**Total B-roll:** 4-5 quick cuts, 1 sec each, during the 60-90 sec video.

## Editing (free tools)

- **CapCut** (mobile + desktop) — TikTok's editor, free, easy
- **InShot** (mobile) — simple, fast
- **VN Video Editor** (mobile) — more features

### Basic edit steps

1. Import clips (your talking + B-roll)
2. Trim talking clips to clean takes
3. Add B-roll cuts (every 5-10 sec, 1 sec each)
4. Add text overlay: "Sarah Roig — Enki Store" at end
5. Add music (optional — quiet instrumental OR trending)
6. Add captions (auto or manual)
7. Export vertical 9:16, 1080p

**Total edit time:** 1-2 hours for first version.
