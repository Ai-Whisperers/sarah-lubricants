## 3 Visual Directions

### Direction 1: Playful Pop (RECOMMENDED)

**Style:**
- Soft pastels (rose, peach, lavender)
- Rounded sans-serif typography
- Friendly geometric icon (curves, no sharp angles)
- Sense of warmth + approachability
- Discreet but not boring

**Mood:** "Cool friend who happens to know a lot about pleasure"

**Color palette:**
- Primary: Rose #E8A4C5 (or dusty pink)
- Secondary: Cream #F5EBE0
- Accent: Deep plum #4A2840
- Text: Charcoal #2A2730

**Typography:**
- Display: Quicksand, Poppins, or Nunito (rounded)
- Body: Inter, Open Sans (clean)

**Icon ideas:**
- Abstract wave (suggesting flow, pleasure, fluidity)
- Crescent moon (suggesting nighttime, intimacy)
- Letter "E" or "K" stylized
- Stylized flower (rose, magnolia)
- Combination mark: wordmark + small icon

**Application:**
- Kinky product cards in WA
- Instagram posts (carousel + reels)
- Packaging stickers
- Product cards on website

### Direction 2: Premium Kink (alternative)

**Style:**
- Dark + moody palette (black, deep purple, gold accent)
- Thin elegant typography (serif + sans pairing)
- Sophisticated, almost luxury-brand feel
- More "Lelo" / "Dame" aesthetic

**Mood:** "Treat yourself — you deserve the best"

**Color palette:**
- Primary: Deep black #0E0D12
- Accent: Gold #C9A961 (luxury)
- Secondary: Burgundy #6B1C2A
- Text: Cream #F0E8D8

**Typography:**
- Display: Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond (serif elegance)
- Body: Montserrat, Lato (clean)

**Icon ideas:**
- Geometric mark (hexagon, diamond, line drawing)
- Letter "E" with elegant serif
- Stylized keyhole
- Minimal line drawing

**Application:**
- Premium positioning
- Higher AOV products (SensBomb, perfumes)
- Gift-ready bundles

### Direction 3: Minimal Naughty (alternative)

**Style:**
- Black + white + single accent color
- Bold typography, very simple
- Almost editorial / magazine feel
- Sex-positive without being explicit

**Mood:** "Unapologetic, modern, real"

**Color palette:**
- Primary: Black #0E0D12
- Accent: Hot pink #FF3366 (or neon orange)
- Text: Black on white

**Typography:**
- Display: Helvetica Bold, Inter Extra Bold
- Body: Inter Regular

**Icon ideas:**
- Simple geometric mark
- Bold initial letter
- Single line drawing of object

**Application:**
- Bold marketing materials
- Story highlights
- Younger demographic (18–25)

## My recommendation

**Direction 1: Playful Pop** — fits Sarah's existing Enki brand warmth, the Kinky sub-section feels natural, and the palette aligns with the wellness/sensual (not explicit) positioning.

Direction 1 also matches Sarah's existing IG aesthetic (assuming she has product photography that's warm/light).

## Don't use

- ❌ Generic "adult store" red/black palette (clichéd, screams cheap)
- ❌ Cursive script fonts (hard to read, dated)
- ❌ Cartoonish illustrations (juvenile)
- ❌ Explicit imagery (nudity, genitalia — Meta shadowban)
- ❌ Too many fonts (max 2: display + body)
- ❌ Complex gradients (don't print well)

## DIY option (no designer budget)

**Tools:**
- Canva (free): https://www.canva.com — has logo templates
- Figma (free): https://www.figma.com — vector design
- Google Fonts: https://fonts.google.com

**Steps:**
1. Pick a font from the typography recommendations
2. Pick 2-3 colors from the palette
3. Type "Enki" (or "Kinky") in a simple composition
4. Add a small icon (wave, crescent moon, or letter mark)
5. Export at multiple sizes

**Cost:** Gs 0 (vs. Gs 1–3M for designer)

**Limitation:** Less unique, more "template-feel." Acceptable for launch, replace with custom design at Month 6+.
