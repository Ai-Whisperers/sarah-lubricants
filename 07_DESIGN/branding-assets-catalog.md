### Secondary (accent) palette

| Color | Hex | Use |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Sage Green | #B8C5A6 | Wellness tags |
| Soft Lavender | #C7B8EA | Sleep / relaxation |
| Warm Gold | #D4A857 | Premium accents |
| Dusty Pink | #D89BAA | Soft variant of Enki Rose |

### Color usage rules

- ✅ **60-30-10 rule** — 60% neutral (white/cream/charcoal), 30% primary (Rose), 10% accent (Kinky Plum or secondary)
- ✅ **Dark mode:** Cream background with Charcoal text (easier on eyes)
- ✅ **Photo overlays:** Use Cream at 80% opacity
- ❌ **Don't mix Rose + Plum as primary + secondary** — too pink, looks "girly" not "modern"
- ❌ **Don't use saturated colors** (e.g. #FF0000) — feels cheap

## Typography

### Primary fonts (Google Fonts, free)

| Font | Use | Weights | Fallback |
|------|-----|---------|----------|
| **Quicksand** | Headlines, subheadlines, CTAs | 400, 500, 700 | Poppins, system sans-serif |
| **Inter** | Body text, descriptions | 400, 500, 600 | system-ui, -apple-system |

### Typography scale

| Level | Font | Size | Weight | Use |
|-------|------|-----:|--------:|-----|
| **H1** | Quicksand | 36-48px | 700 | Page titles |
| **H2** | Quicksand | 28-32px | 700 | Section headers |
| **H3** | Quicksand | 22-26px | 500 | Sub-sections |
| **H4** | Inter | 18-20px | 600 | Card titles |
| **Body** | Inter | 16-18px | 400 | Paragraphs |
| **Caption** | Inter | 13-14px | 400 | Small text, tags |
| **Button** | Quicksand | 16-18px | 500 | CTAs |

### Typography rules

- ✅ Use Quicksand for emotional / playful
- ✅ Use Inter for informational / professional
- ✅ Always use at least 16px for body (mobile readability)
- ❌ Don't use cursive / script fonts (illegible)
- ❌ Don't use more than 2 fonts per design

## Photography style guide

### Brand photography principles

| Element | Standard |
|---------|----------|
| **Lighting** | Soft natural light (window) preferred. Warm tone (3500-4000K). |
| **Background** | Cream, soft pink, white marble, light wood. Never cluttered. |
| **Focus** | Product in focus, background soft (bokeh). |
| **Composition** | Rule of thirds. Negative space. |
| **Color grade** | Warm + soft + slightly desaturated. |
| **Props** | Towels, candles, flowers, soft fabrics. Never show packaging labels. |

### Product photography setup

**Setup (DIY):**
- White poster board or foam board (background)
- Window with diffused light (curtain)
- Phone on tripod
- White poster board as "fill light" (opposite side)

**Setup (with budget, Gs 200K):**
- Small lightbox (Asunción: Photography stores, MercadoLibre)
- Phone tripod with phone holder
- White seamless background

**Per-product shots needed:**
- ✅ Front (product visible)
- ✅ Back (ingredients / label)
- ✅ In-use (hand holding, optional)
- ✅ With prop (next to a flower / candle)
- ✅ Lifestyle (in bathroom, bedroom — discrete)

### Don'ts in photography

- ❌ Don't show packaging labels (privacy)
- ❌ Don't show full address on any visible paper
- ❌ Don't use overly sexualized imagery (Meta shadowban risk)
- ❌ Don't show explicit content (DINAVISA + regulatory)
- ❌ Don't use stock photos with people (feels fake)

## Verbal identity (copy voice)

### Enki Store voice

**Tone:** Warm, honest, like a friend giving advice.
**Sentence length:** Short, conversational. Max 20 words per sentence.
**Avoid:** Corporate jargon, formal phrases.
**Use:** "Vos", "te", direct address.

**Examples:**

❌ "Estimados clientes, nos complace presentarles nuestro nuevo producto..."
✅ "Hola! Te cuento sobre el nuevo Bitchie que acabamos de recibir..."

❌ "La satisfacción de nuestros clientes es nuestra prioridad."
✅ "Si algo no te gusta, escribime y lo resolvemos."

### Kinky voice

**Tone:** Slightly more playful + sensual, but still discreet.
**Sentence length:** Even shorter. Quick reads.
**Avoid:** Explicit language, vulgar words.
**Use:** Emojis sparingly (💕, 🌹, 🔥).

**Examples:**

❌ "Estimado cliente, le ofrecemos una amplia gama de productos íntimos..."
✅ "Probá Bitchie — spray que te va a volar la cabeza 🌹"

❌ "Producto recomendado para mejorar su experiencia íntima."
✅ "¿Buscás algo para vos o para regalar? Te ayudo a elegir."
