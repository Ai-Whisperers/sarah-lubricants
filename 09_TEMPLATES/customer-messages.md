## Tone guidelines

| Tone | When |
|------|------|
| Warm + casual | Welcome, thank-you, follow-up |
| Professional + brief | Order confirm, shipping, payment |
| Empathetic | Complaints, delays, OOS |
| Educational | Product questions, first-time buyers |
| Brief + direct | Repeat customers who know the products |

**Always:**
- ✅ Emojis sparingly (1–3 per message)
- ✅ Customer's name once or twice
- ✅ Clear next step ("¿Confirmamos?")
- ✅ No pushy language
- ❌ Never medical claims ("vas a tener orgasmos múltiples")
- ❌ Never shame the customer ("es muy fácil de usar")
- ❌ Never compare negatively to competitors
