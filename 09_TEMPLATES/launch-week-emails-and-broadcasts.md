## Tone reminders

- ✅ **Warm, personal** — Sarah is the face
- ✅ **Educational** — explain why, not just what
- ✅ **Discrete** — never overshare or use explicit words
- ✅ **Action-oriented** — every email has a CTA
- ✅ **Spanish (PY)** — vos, tenés, querés, etc.
- ❌ **Never pushy** — "buy now or lose forever"
- ❌ **Never clinical** — these are wellness products, not medicine
- ❌ **Never corporate** — Sarah is a real person, not a brand
