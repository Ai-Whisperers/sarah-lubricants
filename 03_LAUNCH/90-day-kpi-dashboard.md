## Dashboard structure (5 categories of KPIs)

### 1. Acquisition KPIs (how customers find us)

| KPI | Definition | Target | Tool |
|-----|-----------|-------:|------|
| **IG impressions** | Total views of IG content | 10K/week | IG analytics |
| **IG reach** | Unique accounts reached | 5K/week | IG analytics |
| **IG engagement rate** | (likes + comments + saves + shares) / followers × 100 | >3% | IG analytics |
| **IG new followers** | Net new followers per week | +100/week | IG analytics |
| **Bio link clicks** | Clicks to WA or subdomain | 200/week | UTM tracked |
| **TikTok views** (Month 3+) | Total video views | 5K/video | TikTok analytics |
| **Email list growth** | New Brevo subscribers | +30/week | Brevo |
| **B2B outreach visits** | In-person shop visits | 2-3/week | Manual log |

### 2. Conversion KPIs (how they become customers)

| KPI | Definition | Target | Tool |
|-----|-----------|-------:|------|
| **WA messages received** | Total inbound WA messages | 30/week | WA Business |
| **WA → inquiry → order rate** | % of WA messages that convert to order | >30% | Manual log |
| **Average response time** | Avg time from customer message to Sarah's reply | <1 hour | WA Business |
| **Cart abandonment** | % of customers who asked price but didn't order | <50% | Manual log |
| **AOV** | Average order value | >Gs 130K | Manual log |
| **First-order rate** | % of IG impressions → first order | >0.5% | Calculated |
| **Bundle attach rate** | % of orders that include a bundle | >25% | Manual log |

### 3. Retention KPIs (how they stay customers)

| KPI | Definition | Target | Tool |
|-----|-----------|-------:|------|
| **Repeat purchase rate (30d)** | % of customers who buy again within 30 days | >15% | Manual log |
| **Repeat purchase rate (90d)** | % who buy again within 90 days | >25% | Manual log |
| **Customer LTV (90d)** | Avg revenue per customer in first 90 days | >Gs 150K | Calculated |
| **Email engagement** | Open rate / click rate | >25% / >3% | Brevo |
| **WA response rate** | % of WA broadcasts opened | >70% | WA Business |
| **Referral rate** | % of customers who refer someone | >15% | Manual ask |

### 4. Operational KPIs (how well we execute)

| KPI | Definition | Target | Tool |
|-----|-----------|-------:|------|
| **Orders processed per day** | Avg daily order volume | 5-10 by Month 3 | Manual |
| **Pack + ship time** | Avg time from order to ship | <24 hours | Manual |
| **Pack + ship time (full)** | Order to delivery | <72 hours Asunción | Manual |
| **Returns rate** | % of orders returned | <5% | Returns SOP |
| **Complaints per month** | # of complaints received | <3/month | Returns SOP |
| **Inventory accuracy** | % of orders fulfilled without stockout | >98% | Manual |
| **Customer satisfaction (NPS)** | Would recommend? | >40 NPS | Quarterly survey |

### 5. Financial KPIs (the bottom line)

| KPI | Definition | Target | Tool |
|-----|-----------|-------:|------|
| **Monthly revenue** | Total sales | Gs 2.6M (M1) → Gs 14M (M6) | Manual log |
| **Monthly COGS** | Total cost of goods sold | 38% of revenue | Calculator |
| **Gross margin** | Revenue - COGS | >49% | Calculator |
| **Monthly net profit** | After all costs | >0 by Month 3 | Cash flow model |
| **Cumulative cash** | Total cash in bank | >0 by Month 6 | Cash flow model |
| **Burn rate** | Monthly cash burn (if negative) | <Gs 5M | Cash flow model |
| **CAC** | Customer Acquisition Cost | <Gs 30K | Calculated |
| **LTV/CAC ratio** | LTV divided by CAC | >3:1 | Calculated |

## Variance alerts

When any KPI deviates by >30% from target for 2 consecutive weeks:

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Orders down 30%+ | Check: stock, IG engagement, WA response time, competitor launch |
| AOV down 30%+ | Check: bundle pricing, bundle visibility, mix shift |
| Returns up 30%+ | Check: product quality, shipping damage, customer expectations |
| New followers down 30%+ | Check: IG content quality, hashtag strategy, posting cadence |
| Cash burn > Gs 1M/mo | Check: model旅行者 viability, despachante costs, marketing spend |
