Logo Design Brief — Enki Store + Kinky Sub-brand

Date: June 26, 2026 Audience: Designer / agency (or DIY via Canva/Figma/Adobe) Goal: Visual identity for Enki Store (parent) + Kinky (sub-brand) Style direction: Modern + wellness-forward + slightly sensual (not explicit)

Brand context

Enki Store is Sarah's existing brand (IG @enkistore, 20K followers, RUC 4978694-6). "Kinky" is a sub-brand / sección interna inside Enki for intimate-wellness products.

Voice: Juguetón + discreto + 18+ sutil + moderno. (Per 01_RESEARCH/brand-identity/enki-store-assessment.md)

Tagline candidates: - "Placer sin etiquetas" - "Para vos, en privado" - "Cuidá tu momento" - "Bienestar íntimo, sin complejos"

Deliverables

1. Enki Store logo (parent brand, primary)

Use cases: - IG profile photo - WhatsApp Business profile - Packaging (when Kinky sub-brand also present) - facturas / business documents - Email signature

Specs: - Primary logo (full color) - Reverse logo (white on dark) - Monochrome (black on white) - Icon-only mark (for favicon, app icon, profile circle) - SVG + PNG (high-res) + JPG

File sizes needed: - 1024×1024 px (IG profile, square crop) - 500×500 px (WA Business) - 64×64 px (favicon) - Print-ready vector (SVG, AI)

2. Kinky sub-mark (used alongside Enki on intimate products only)

Use cases: - Sub-brand badge on Kinky section of IG - Sub-brand stamp on intimate-only packaging - Kinky hashtag header

Specs: - Wordmark "Kinky" only (not a full logo — Enki is the parent) - Optional icon symbol (see direction 3 below) - 3 sizes minimum

3. Co-branded packaging lockup

Use case: When Sexitive + Enki appear together on packaging

Layout:

[Sexitive logo]   ×   [Enki logo]
       "Distribuido por Enki Store en Paraguay"

3 Visual Directions

Direction 1: Playful Pop (RECOMMENDED)

Style: - Soft pastels (rose, peach, lavender) - Rounded sans-serif typography - Friendly geometric icon (curves, no sharp angles) - Sense of warmth + approachability - Discreet but not boring

Mood: "Cool friend who happens to know a lot about pleasure"

Color palette: - Primary: Rose #E8A4C5 (or dusty pink) - Secondary: Cream #F5EBE0 - Accent: Deep plum #4A2840 - Text: Charcoal #2A2730

Typography: - Display: Quicksand, Poppins, or Nunito (rounded) - Body: Inter, Open Sans (clean)

Icon ideas: - Abstract wave (suggesting flow, pleasure, fluidity) - Crescent moon (suggesting nighttime, intimacy) - Letter "E" or "K" stylized - Stylized flower (rose, magnolia) - Combination mark: wordmark + small icon

Application: - Kinky product cards in WA - Instagram posts (carousel + reels) - Packaging stickers - Product cards on website

Direction 2: Premium Kink (alternative)

Style: - Dark + moody palette (black, deep purple, gold accent) - Thin elegant typography (serif + sans pairing) - Sophisticated, almost luxury-brand feel - More "Lelo" / "Dame" aesthetic

Mood: "Treat yourself — you deserve the best"

Color palette: - Primary: Deep black #0E0D12 - Accent: Gold #C9A961 (luxury) - Secondary: Burgundy #6B1C2A - Text: Cream #F0E8D8

Typography: - Display: Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond (serif elegance) - Body: Montserrat, Lato (clean)

Icon ideas: - Geometric mark (hexagon, diamond, line drawing) - Letter "E" with elegant serif - Stylized keyhole - Minimal line drawing

Application: - Premium positioning - Higher AOV products (SensBomb, perfumes) - Gift-ready bundles

Direction 3: Minimal Naughty (alternative)

Style: - Black + white + single accent color - Bold typography, very simple - Almost editorial / magazine feel - Sex-positive without being explicit

Mood: "Unapologetic, modern, real"

Color palette: - Primary: Black #0E0D12 - Accent: Hot pink #FF3366 (or neon orange) - Text: Black on white

Typography: - Display: Helvetica Bold, Inter Extra Bold - Body: Inter Regular

Icon ideas: - Simple geometric mark - Bold initial letter - Single line drawing of object

Application: - Bold marketing materials - Story highlights - Younger demographic (18–25)

My recommendation

Direction 1: Playful Pop — fits Sarah's existing Enki brand warmth, the Kinky sub-section feels natural, and the palette aligns with the wellness/sensual (not explicit) positioning.

Direction 1 also matches Sarah's existing IG aesthetic (assuming she has product photography that's warm/light).

Don't use

  • ❌ Generic "adult store" red/black palette (clichéd, screams cheap)
  • ❌ Cursive script fonts (hard to read, dated)
  • ❌ Cartoonish illustrations (juvenile)
  • ❌ Explicit imagery (nudity, genitalia — Meta shadowban)
  • ❌ Too many fonts (max 2: display + body)
  • ❌ Complex gradients (don't print well)
