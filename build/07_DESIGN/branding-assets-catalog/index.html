Color usage rules

  • ✅ 60-30-10 rule — 60% neutral (white/cream/charcoal), 30% primary (Rose), 10% accent (Kinky Plum or secondary)
  • ✅ Dark mode: Cream background with Charcoal text (easier on eyes)
  • ✅ Photo overlays: Use Cream at 80% opacity
  • ❌ Don't mix Rose + Plum as primary + secondary — too pink, looks "girly" not "modern"
  • ❌ Don't use saturated colors (e.g. #FF0000) — feels cheap

Typography rules

  • ✅ Use Quicksand for emotional / playful
  • ✅ Use Inter for informational / professional
  • ✅ Always use at least 16px for body (mobile readability)
  • ❌ Don't use cursive / script fonts (illegible)
  • ❌ Don't use more than 2 fonts per design

Photography style guide

Brand photography principles

Element Standard
Lighting Soft natural light (window) preferred. Warm tone (3500-4000K).
Background Cream, soft pink, white marble, light wood. Never cluttered.
Focus Product in focus, background soft (bokeh).
Composition Rule of thirds. Negative space.
Color grade Warm + soft + slightly desaturated.
Props Towels, candles, flowers, soft fabrics. Never show packaging labels.

Product photography setup

Setup (DIY): - White poster board or foam board (background) - Window with diffused light (curtain) - Phone on tripod - White poster board as "fill light" (opposite side)

Setup (with budget, Gs 200K): - Small lightbox (Asunción: Photography stores, MercadoLibre) - Phone tripod with phone holder - White seamless background

Per-product shots needed: - ✅ Front (product visible) - ✅ Back (ingredients / label) - ✅ In-use (hand holding, optional) - ✅ With prop (next to a flower / candle) - ✅ Lifestyle (in bathroom, bedroom — discrete)

Don'ts in photography

  • ❌ Don't show packaging labels (privacy)
  • ❌ Don't show full address on any visible paper
  • ❌ Don't use overly sexualized imagery (Meta shadowban risk)
  • ❌ Don't show explicit content (DINAVISA + regulatory)
  • ❌ Don't use stock photos with people (feels fake)

Verbal identity (copy voice)

Enki Store voice

Tone: Warm, honest, like a friend giving advice. Sentence length: Short, conversational. Max 20 words per sentence. Avoid: Corporate jargon, formal phrases. Use: "Vos", "te", direct address.

Examples:

❌ "Estimados clientes, nos complace presentarles nuestro nuevo producto..." ✅ "Hola! Te cuento sobre el nuevo Bitchie que acabamos de recibir..."

❌ "La satisfacción de nuestros clientes es nuestra prioridad." ✅ "Si algo no te gusta, escribime y lo resolvemos."

Kinky voice

Tone: Slightly more playful + sensual, but still discreet. Sentence length: Even shorter. Quick reads. Avoid: Explicit language, vulgar words. Use: Emojis sparingly (💕, 🌹, 🔥).

Examples:

❌ "Estimado cliente, le ofrecemos una amplia gama de productos íntimos..." ✅ "Probá Bitchie — spray que te va a volar la cabeza 🌹"

❌ "Producto recomendado para mejorar su experiencia íntima." ✅ "¿Buscás algo para vos o para regalar? Te ayudo a elegir."
