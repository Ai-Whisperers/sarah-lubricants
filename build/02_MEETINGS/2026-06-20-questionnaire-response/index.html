The 3 hard blockers

🔴 BLOCKER 1 — Pink Sexy Pill is incompatible with < Gs 10M capital

  • Pink Sexy Pill = dietary supplement (ginseng, L-Arginina, etc.) under DINAVISA.
  • DINAVISA supplement registration: Gs 4–8M, 90–180 days (per 01_RESEARCH/legal-regulatory/dinavisa-import-requirements.md).
  • Sarah's 90-day capital budget: < Gs 10M total.
  • Even if Sarah spends the entire budget on DINAVISA, she has nothing left for stock, freight, packaging, or marketing.

Options: - (a) Drop Pink Sexy Pill from launch. Launch with Bitchie + XXX For Her (both are cosmetics, not supplements — Gs 1.5–4M NSO registration, 30–60 days). Pursue DINAVISA supplement in parallel with revenue. - (b) Find capital beyond the Gs 10M she declared (e.g., from existing business). - (c) Use the Sexitive AR manager relationship to obtain pre-cleared ANMAT paperwork, which shortens DINAVISA timeline.

My recommendation: (a). Launch with the cosmetic pair. De-risk by validating market first. When 3 SKUs are selling, allocate Gs 5–8M from revenue to register Pink Sexy Pill. Add it to catalog 3–6 months in.

🟡 BLOCKER 2 — "Kinky Store" carries trademark + ad-policy risk

  • Sarah says the brand is already registered at DINAPI. Need to verify:
  • Which classes? (3 = cosmetics, 5 = pharmaceuticals, 35 = retail, 41 = education)
  • Is it Sarah's registration, or a third party who might own "Kinky Store" in PY already?
  • The word "Kinky" triggers Meta/Google/TikTok content filters aggressively. Even if the site is clean, paid ads will be hard to run.
  • "Kinky" is also polarizing in Spanish PY — some read it as playful, others as crude. May hurt trust with Sarah's target audience (women 25–45, parejas).

Recommended actions: - Sarah to send DINAPI registration screenshot (number + classes + titular). - Check domain kinkystore.com availability + variants (kinkystore.com.py, kinkystoreparaguay.com, kinkylab.com). - Plan A: Kinky Store, A-class registration, with subdomain kinky.paragu-ai.com for fast launch while .com is acquired. - Plan B: variant name (Kinklab, Kink & Co, Kink Studio, Kinkhy) if Plan A has friction.

🟡 BLOCKER 3 — "Todos" target audience = no positioning

  • All 5 PY competitors (sexshop.com.py, Rivia, Íntimos Placeres, As Bajo La Manga, Sex Shop Paraguay) have a defined audience:
  • SexShop.com.py → "cadena tradicional"
  • Rivia → "tu sex shop de confianza" (modern, trust)
  • Íntimos Placeres → lubricante specialist
  • As Bajo La Manga → "sensualidad y romanticismo"
  • Sex Shop Paraguay → "discreto, dos tiendas"
  • "Todos" is not a positioning — it's the absence of one.
  • With 3 SKUs and < Gs 10M capital, Sarah can't out-spend the incumbents. She has to out-position them.
  • Tone is "juguetón/divertido" (playful) — that implies young, fun, social-media-native. A natural audience is "parejas 25–35 urbanas, Asunción/CDE, primera compra online de este tipo de producto."

My recommendation: sharpen to "Parejas + mujeres 25–35 urbanas, Asunción primero, expand to CDE en mes 3." That gives a copy direction, a content direction, and a media-buy direction.
