Sexitive Wholesale — How to Buy from Argentina

Source: Sexitive wholesale channel at https://clientes.sexitive.com + direct contact channels. Last verified: June 19, 2026

Two ways to access Sexitive

Path A — Sexitive wholesale (clientes.sexitive.com)

The B2B portal at clientes.sexitive.com is Sexitive's dedicated wholesale channel. Visible on the catalog:

  • Contact: Lima 1548, Martinez, Buenos Aires · Tel (5411) 7500-7969 · WhatsApp +54 9 11 7100-5976 (wholesale line, distinct from consumer lines)

Process to open a wholesale account:

  1. Sarah emails or WhatsApps Sexitive AR at the wholesale contact requesting a "cuenta mayorista."
  2. Sexitive requests: RUC of Sarah's PY business (or CUIT if Sarah has AR fiscal entity), proof of business activity, possibly a reference from a known AR wholesaler.
  3. Once approved, Sexitive assigns a price list and a sales rep.
  4. Sarah places orders via the wholesale site or via the assigned rep.

What we don't know publicly: - MOQ per order (likely 50–100 units per SKU, or minimum order value). - Payment terms (probably upfront wire transfer; some accounts get 30-day terms after 3+ orders). - Wholesale discount from retail (estimated 30–50% off AR retail, see 01_RESEARCH/market/sexitive-catalog-master.md). - Whether Sexitive ships to PY or only to AR addresses (the latter would require a freight forwarder).

Path B — AR MercadoLibre / retail arbitrage

Sarah can also buy Sexitive products off MercadoLibre AR at retail prices and reship to PY. This is arbitrage, not a sustainable business — margins are thin (10–30%) and shipping costs are high. Use only for market testing, not for ongoing supply.

If Sarah uses Path B for the first 5–10 orders to validate the market, then transitions to Path A for ongoing supply, that's a valid 60–90 day strategy.

Red flags to watch for in Sexitive's response

Red flag Meaning Action
"Ya tenemos distribuidor en Paraguay" Sarah is too late for Model 1 Pivot to Model 2 or 3
"MOQ es USD 50K" Capital-intensive, not Model 1 starting point Negotiate smaller first order
"No emitimos certificado de origen" +14–18% duty, kills margins Insist or walk away
"Solo despachamos dentro de Argentina" Need a freight forwarder in AR Engage one (see logistics doc)
"Pago 100% anticipado" Standard, but check if 30-day terms possible after order 2–3 Negotiate after trust built
"No vendemos a Paraguay" Sexitive has distribution restrictions Pivot to Model 2 (resell from AR via ML) or Model 3 (private label)

If more than 1 red flag fires, Model 1 is not viable. Fall back to Model 2 or 3.

  • Wait 5 business days, then send a polite follow-up.
  • If still no response after 10 business days, call (5411) 7500-7969.
  • If still nothing, pivot to Path B (AR MercadoLibre) for initial stock + start the Model 3 private-label search.
  • Document the non-response in 02_MEETINGS/ for future reference.
