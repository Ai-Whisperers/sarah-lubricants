What NOT to do

  • ❌ Don't use medical claims ("cura la disfunción")
  • ❌ Don't use graphic language
  • ❌ Don't copy competitor content
  • ❌ Don't write generic AI-sounding posts (always add personal voice)
  • ❌ Don't publish without a CTA to WA
  • ❌ Don't skip the FAQ section (huge SEO boost)
  • ❌ Don't forget mobile optimization (70%+ of PY traffic is mobile)
