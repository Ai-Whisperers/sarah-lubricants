Top finding

Paraguayan TikTok around intimacy is fragmented across 3 content categories:

  1. Couple/lifestyle content (largest, 7.9M+ posts in "Pareja 2026" trend)
  2. Wellness/self-care content (small but growing, mostly female-focused)

For Sarah: The opportunity is in Pillar 1 (Couple/lifestyle) with wellness framing. This is the most permissive + has the biggest audience + aligns with Kinky's "juguetón + discreto" tone.

TikTok ad policy for intimate content

Allowed on TikTok: - ✅ Product demonstrations (no nudity) - ✅ Wellness content (lubricants as "personal care") - ✅ Couple content (intimate but not explicit) - ✅ "How to use" tutorials (tasteful) - ✅ Customer testimonials

Not allowed on TikTok: - ❌ Nudity - ❌ Explicit sexual content - ❌ Pornographic language - ❌ "How to have sex" instructional

Content do's and don'ts

✅ DO

  • Show products tastefully (in hands, on a tray, with props)
  • Use trending sounds (TikTok algorithm boost)
  • Engage with comments within 1 hour
  • Use Sarah's voice (personal = relatable)
  • Show diversity (different people, couples, ages)
  • Film in natural light
  • Use caption overlays (sound-off viewers)

❌ DON'T

  • Show nudity (shadowban)
  • Use explicit language
  • Show Sexitive brand labels (privacy)
  • Mention "sex shop" or "adult store"
  • Use prohibited hashtags (#sex, #porn, #nsfw)
  • Buy followers (kills engagement)
  • Post without hook (lost in algorithm)
