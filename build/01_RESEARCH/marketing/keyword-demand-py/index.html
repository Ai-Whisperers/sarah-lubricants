Date: June 26, 2026 Sources: Google Trends PY, web search keyword discovery, competitor site content Use case: SEO content calendar, IG hashtag strategy, paid ad (where allowed) keyword selection

Headline finding

PY intimate-wellness search volume is LOW and SEASONAL: - Peak: February (Valentine's Day + Day of Love Feb 14) - Secondary peak: December (Christmas gifting) - Tertiary: September (Day of Love Sep 14, Mother's Day May 15) - Low: January, June–August (no demand driver)

Implication: Content calendar should front-load Q4 + Feb. Inventory build-up should target Nov–Jan for Feb peak.
