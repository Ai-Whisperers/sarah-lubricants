What NOT to do

  • ❌ Don't pitch influencers in explicit-adjacent niches (will trigger Meta filters)
  • ❌ Don't promise guaranteed results (regulatory risk)
  • ❌ Don't send products without prior approval + signed terms
  • ❌ Don't expect "go viral" — micro-influencers convert via trust, not reach
  • ❌ Don't pitch couples explicitly ("do it with your partner") — frame as personal wellness
