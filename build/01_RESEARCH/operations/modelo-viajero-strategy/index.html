Modelo Viajero Strategy (Personal Luggage Import)

Date: June 26, 2026 Context: Sarah's June 17 trip to Sexitive AR brought goods as personal luggage, not formal import. This saved Gs 1.5–4M in DINAVISA + despachante fees.

Decision needed: Does Sarah continue with this model for the first 3+ shipments, or formalize immediately?

What "modelo viajero" means

Definition: Bringing commercial-quantity goods into PY via personal luggage on a return flight from AR (or any country).

Legal framework (PY): - Aduana PY allows each traveler 1 personal-effects declaration per calendar year, duty-free up to USD 150 (or similar threshold, varies) - Anything above that = taxed + potentially seized - Commercial quantities = red flag for customs

Risk levels:

Quantity brought Risk level Likely outcome
< 5 units/SKU 🟢 Low Personal use, no questions
5–20 units/SKU 🟡 Medium Borderline, may be taxed
20–50 units/SKU 🟠 High Likely flagged as commercial
50+ units/SKU 🔴 Very high Confiscation risk + retroactive DINAVISA

Sarah's June 17 trip: Unknown quantity, but assumed < 20 units/SKU based on WhatsApp evidence.

Recommended strategy

Phase 1 (Months 1–3): Hybrid modelo viajero + formal

  1. Shipment 1 (modelo viajero): Sarah goes to AR, buys 30u of each launch SKU. Test market. No DINAVISA paperwork needed for < 30 units.
  2. In parallel: Engage contador + despachante. File DINAVISA NSO for 3 launch SKUs.
  3. Shipment 2 (modelo viajero, ~6 weeks later): If launch metrics good, do another trip with 60u of each SKU.
  4. Shipment 3+ (formal, after DINAVISA approved): Switch to formal freight once DINAVISA is in hand.

Phase 2 (Months 4+): Fully formal

  • Once DINAVISA NSO is approved, formal imports are no longer risky
  • Use DHL consolidated freight (cheaper per unit at scale)
  • Modelo viajero only for opportunistic top-ups

Risk mitigation for modelo viajero

Quantity discipline

  • Hard limit: < 30 units per SKU per trip
  • Hard limit: < 50 total units per trip
  • Documentation: Keep receipt from Sexitive AR (proves quantity)
  • Packaging: Use original Sexitive packaging (looks like personal use)

Declaration strategy

  • First trip per year: Use the personal effects declaration (USD 150 threshold)
  • Subsequent trips: Declare nothing if under radar, OR declare + pay tax if above threshold
  • Never declare commercial intent (this triggers DINAVISA inspection + retroactive penalties)

Trip frequency

  • Max: 1 trip per quarter (4/year) to stay under "frequent flyer" scrutiny
  • Combine with: Personal trips (family visit, vacation) when possible — cheaper

Documentation to keep

  • ✅ Receipt from Sexitive AR (proves wholesale purchase)
  • ✅ Flight booking confirmation
  • ✅ Hotel reservation
  • ✅ Photos of goods (in case customs asks)

Red flags to avoid

  • ❌ Don't bring the same products 3+ times in 12 months (looks like commercial)
  • ❌ Don't bring products in commercial-looking packaging (cartons with barcodes, etc.)
  • ❌ Don't bring > 100 units total per trip (over the line)
  • ❌ Don't connect the same flight route repeatedly without a personal reason
  • ❌ Don't bring product samples for multiple businesses at once

When to formalize (transition triggers)

Switch to formal import when ANY of these happen:

  1. ✅ DINAVISA NSO approved — makes formal import cheap + legal
  2. ✅ Order volume > 100 units/month — modelo viajero can't scale
  3. ✅ Customs flags a shipment — even one seizure = stop
  4. ✅ B2B wholesale orders > 50 units/SKU — commercial scale needs paperwork
  5. ✅ Sarah's contador advises formalization — tax compliance
  6. ✅ 6+ months of modelo viajero — diminishing returns + rising risk

Tax implications

Modelo旅行者

  • ✅ No IVA import (personal effects)
  • ✅ No DINAVISA fees
  • ⚠️ But you DO owe income tax on resale profits (IRP)
  • ⚠️ Sales should still be declared to SET

Formal import

  • ✅ Same income tax (IRP)
  • ➕ IVA import 10% paid (recoverable as IVA crédito fiscal if formal business)
  • ➕ DINAVISA paid (one-time per SKU)
  • ➕ Despachante paid (per shipment)

Key insight: Modelo viajero saves 28–32% on landed cost. But once you formalize + register properly, you can recover IVA crédito fiscal on inputs (which is a future benefit modelo viajero doesn't get).
