LatAm Supplier Backup Matrix — Beyond Sexitive AR

Date: June 26, 2026 Purpose: Document alternative suppliers in case Sexitive AR relationship breaks Scope: AR, BR, UY, BO wholesale distributors

Why this matters: Sexitive AR is Sarah's sole supplier. If the relationship breaks (price hike, supply issue, gerente leaves, quality dispute), she has zero backup. This doc gives her a tested list of alternatives.

Tier 1 — Direct Sexitive competitors (AR wholesale)

1. SexShopMayorista.com (Sexitive AR dropship program)

URL: https://www.sexshopmayorista.com/ Background: Operated by Sexitive AR. Same brand Sarah is buying from. Different channel (dropship).

Model: Sexitive ships directly to Sarah's customers. No inventory needed.

Pros: - ✅ Same products Sarah already knows - ✅ Sexitive handles shipping + DINAVISA - ✅ Zero capital tied up - ✅ Easy to start

Cons: - ❌ Lower margin (~25-30% net vs. 50% on direct) - ❌ Sexitive branding on package (less discretion) - ❌ No B2B wholesale (dropship is DTC-only) - ❌ No DINAVISA in Sarah's name (no brand value) - ❌ Single point of failure (Sexitive controls)

When to use: As a backup if Sexitive direct fails. Bridge while finding new supplier.
