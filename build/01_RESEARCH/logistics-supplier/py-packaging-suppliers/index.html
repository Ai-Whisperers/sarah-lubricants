Recommended packaging stack (per launch order)

Discrete unbranded outer box

  • Size: 20×15×10 cm (single SKU) or 25×20×15 cm (trio)
  • Material: Brown corrugated cardboard, no printing
  • MOQ: 100 units
  • Quote target: Gs 2,000–3,500/unit
  • Supplier: Pixxel or Print&Pack

Internal black tissue paper

  • Size: 50×75 cm sheet
  • MOQ: 500 sheets
  • Quote target: Gs 150–300/sheet
  • Supplier: La República or Pixxel

Packaging design specs (brief for designer or imprenta)

Box exterior

  • No logos
  • No product images
  • No brand name visible
  • Brown cardboard only
  • Single-color print OK if needed (sender address only)

Inner tissue paper

  • Black (premium feel)
  • Optional: small "Enki" watermark in corner (subtle)

Bilingual sticker (DINAVISA overlay)

  • Spanish + small English
  • Required fields: Importador (Sarah), RUC, lote, vencimiento, país origen, "uso externo"
  • Color: White background, black text
  • Adhesive: Strong (must survive shipping)

Thank-you card

  • Front: "Gracias por tu compra 💕" + small Enki logo
  • Back: WA contact + @enkistore + brief return policy
  • Full color, both sides
  • Matte lamination (premium feel)
