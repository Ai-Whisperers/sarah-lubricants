DINAVISA Import Requirements — Cosmetics, Supplements, CBD

Authority: DINAVISA (Dirección Nacional de Vigilancia Sanitaria), under MSPBS (Ministerio de Salud Pública y Bienestar Social) Site: https://dinavisa.gov.py Last verified: June 19, 2026

The 4 regulatory buckets for Sexitive's catalog

Sexitive's 12 SKUs fall into 4 different DINAVISA categories. Each has a different registration process, timeline, and cost. Misclassifying a product is the #1 way to lose money at customs.

Bucket 1 — Cosméticos (lubricants, body oils, candles, topical sprays)

Covers: Wet line, more sex, Love Potion, Bitchie spray, XXX For Her, massage candles

Regulatory basis: Resolución DINAVISA N° 371/2024 (clasificación, requisitos, renovación de registros sanitarios y NSO).

Process: 1. Registro Sanitario — product-by-product registration with DINAVISA. - Documents: certificate of free sale from country of origin, formulation, labeling, manufacturing process, stability data. - Sexitive AR must provide these — Sarah cannot import without them. 2. Notificación Sanitaria Obligatoria (NSO) — for some low-risk cosmetics, an NSO may suffice in lieu of full registration (faster, cheaper). Check per-SKU with despachante. 3. Certificado de Libre Venta — issued by ANMAT (Argentina's regulator) for Sexitive's products. Required. 4. Traducción pública — Spanish translation of all labeling, done by a Traductor Público matriculado. 5. Etiquetado conforme a MSPBS — labels may need adjustment to comply with Paraguayan regulations (Spanish language, RUC of importer, local contact).

Timeline: 60–120 days per SKU for full registration; 15–30 days for NSO. Cost estimate: Gs 1.5M – 4M per SKU (full registration); Gs 300K – 800K per SKU (NSO). Confirm with despachante.

Bucket 2 — Suplementos Dietarios (Pink Sexy Pill only)

Covers: Pink Sexy Pill (4 tablets, libido supplement with ginseng, L-Arginina, etc.)

Regulatory basis: Resolución DINAVISA N° 234 (régimen de obtención y renovación de constancia de inscripción sanitaria para suplementos dietarios).

Process: 1. Constancia de Inscripción Sanitaria — separate from cosmetic registration. 2. Composición cualicuantitativa — full ingredient disclosure with doses. 3. Certificado de Libre Venta from ANMAT. 4. Estudio de estabilidad — stability data may be required. 5. Dictamen técnico — DINAVISA technical opinion on formulation.

Timeline: 90–180 days. Longest timeline in the catalog. Cost estimate: Gs 4M – 8M. Most expensive per SKU. Risk: DINAVISA may reject the formulation if it contains ingredients not on the approved list for PY supplements.

Bucket 3 — Cosmético con CBD (My Hemp only)

Covers: My Hemp 15ml CBD body oil.

Regulatory status: Zona gris en Paraguay. CBD topicals are not explicitly regulated: - Not in the supplement category (it's topical, not ingestible). - Not clearly in the cosmetic category because CBD is a novel active. - No clear DINAVISA pathway exists as of June 2026.

Options: 1. Skip My Hemp at launch — lowest-risk option. 2. Apply for cosmetic registration with CBD declared as active — DINAVISA may accept or reject. No precedent. 3. Import as "cosmetic body oil" without declaring CBD on the customs declaration — illegal; if caught at customs, seizure and possible fine. 4. Wait for SENAD (Secretaría Nacional Antidrogas) clarification on CBD cosmetics — could take 6–18 months.

Recommendation: Skip My Hemp at launch. Re-evaluate in 6 months when regulatory landscape may be clearer.

Bucket 4 — Edible-flavored (more sex line)

Covers: more sex — Berries, Chocolate, Dulce de Leche (50ml flavored edible gels)

Regulatory consideration: Edible = food-adjacent. Even though they're sold as lubricants, the "edible" claim may trigger INAN (Instituto Nacional de Alimentación y Nutrición) review.

Likely outcome: Classified as cosmetics with food-grade flavoring, not as food products. Confirm with despachante before launching.

Age-restriction considerations

Paraguayan law does not have a clear minimum age for purchasing intimate products. However:

  • Best practice: state "18+" on packaging, on the site, and in WhatsApp catalog.
  • Use age-gate on the website (pop-up "Confirmo que soy mayor de 18 años").
  • Refuse sales to minors if a customer identifies as such in chat.
  • This protects Sarah from any future regulatory tightening.

What Sarah should ask the despachante (today)

  2. "¿Qué SKUs pueden usar NSO en vez de registro completo?"
