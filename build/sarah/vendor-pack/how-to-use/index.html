How to Use This Pack

Audience: Sarah Roig Goal: Get 3 quotes per vendor type in 1 week, then make decisions

The plan (1 week)

Day Action Time
Monday morning Send Q5 PDF request to Sexitive AR gerente (WhatsApp) 10 min
Monday afternoon Email 3 despachantes (template in outreach-emails.md) 30 min
Tuesday morning Phone 3 contadores (use questions from contador-questions.md) 1 hour
Tuesday afternoon In-person visit to La República S.R.L. for imprenta quote 1 hour
Wednesday Phone 2 couriers (AEX + Mirtrans) 1 hour
Thursday Follow up on all outstanding 30 min
Friday Get all answers, score vendors (use vendor-scoring.md), make decisions 2 hours
Total time ~7 hours over 5 days

Step-by-step instructions

Step 1: Open this folder on your phone/computer

The folder has 10 files. Open the index page (00-index.md) to see them all.

Step 2: Print the questions you need

Before each vendor call or meeting, print the relevant file: - For Sexitive AR gerente: sexitive-ar-questions.md - For despachante: despachante-questions.md - For contador: contador-questions.md - For imprenta: imprentas-questions.md - For courier: courier-questions.md

Step 3: Send outreach (use templates in outreach-emails.md)

Copy-paste the template into WhatsApp or email. Don't customize much — the templates are ready.

Step 4: During the call/meeting

Take notes in the printed file. Fill in the answers as you go.

Step 5: After all vendors respond

Open vendor-scoring.md and fill in the scores. Use the same scoring for each category (despachante, contador, imprenta, courier).

Step 6: Make decision

Pick the highest scorer. If close scores, use judgment (gut feel, references, chemistry).

Step 7: Sign engagement letter

For despachante and contador (imprenta and courier usually don't need formal engagement). Use the template in vendor-conversation-questions.md for key clauses.

Step 8: Send all answers to Erebus

Erebus updates the pricing model, budget, and cash flow projections based on actual numbers.

What NOT to do

  • ❌ Don't send all 7 templates at once (overwhelming)
  • ❌ Don't accept the first quote without comparison
  • ❌ Don't sign without reading engagement letter
  • ❌ Don't pay 100% upfront
  • ❌ Don't hire a vendor without checking references
  • ❌ Don't promise exclusivity to any vendor in first year

What TO do

  • ✅ Send outreach Monday morning
  • ✅ Follow up Thursday if no response
  • ✅ Ask ALL the questions (vendors expect this from serious businesses)
  • ✅ Take notes during calls (you'll forget otherwise)
  • ✅ Compare 3+ candidates per category
  • ✅ Choose based on score + gut feel
  • ✅ Send answers to Erebus for model updates

If something goes wrong

Problem Action
Vendor doesn't respond in 3 days Phone call instead of email/WhatsApp
Vendor gives vague answers Red flag → try next candidate
All candidates score poorly Search more (Google, LinkedIn, referrals)
Vendor asks for 100% upfront Reject → try next
Sarah gets decision paralysis Pick the highest-scoring vendor and move on

After the launch (Month 1+)

  • Re-evaluate vendors monthly (per daily-checklist.md)
  • Look for new vendors as business grows
  • Fire vendors who don't deliver (per vendor-red-flags.md)

Help is available

If you're stuck or overwhelmed: 1. Call Ivan (the founder, your mentor) 2. Message Kiki (sales lead, your primary contact) 3. Send questions to Erebus via GitHub issues

Don't make decisions alone. Use the network.
