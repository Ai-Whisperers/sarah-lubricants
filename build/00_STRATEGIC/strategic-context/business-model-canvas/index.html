Business Model Canvas — Enki Store (Kinky Sub-brand)

Date: June 26, 2026 Format: Osterwalder Business Model Canvas (9 blocks) Status: V1 — based on June 22 decisions + June 26 research

1. Customer Segments

Primary (CS1): Women 25–40 in Asunción urban + Gran Asunción - Looking for intimate wellness products - Comfortable buying via WhatsApp + Instagram - Disposable income for personal care - 20K existing Enki audience + extension to followers of new accounts

Secondary (CS2): Couples 25–45, Asunción + CDE - One partner discovers products, both try - Gift-buying occasions (San Valentín, anniversaries)

Tertiary (CS3): Existing PY sex shop owners (B2B wholesale) - Need private-label / wholesale supplier - 23 shops identified in 01_RESEARCH/market/py-sex-shop-directory.md

Future (CS4): LatAm expansion - BR/AR/UY/BO (per latam-intimate-competitive-landscape.md)

2. Value Propositions

For DTC customers (CS1, CS2): - 🔒 Discrete — Empaque genérico, sin logos, sin marca visible. Nadie sabe qué es. - ✅ Confiable — DINAVISA NSO registrado, ANMAT certificado, import directo de Sexitive AR. - 💬 Personalizado — Atención directa por WhatsApp con Sarah (no chatbot). - 🌟 Curado — Catálogo seleccionado de los mejores productos Sexitive (no vendemos de todo). - 📚 Educativo — Contenido que enseña bienestar íntimo (única marca PY con esto).

For B2B customers (CS3): - 📦 Stock inmediato — Asunción, entrega 24-48h. - 💰 Mayorista directo — Sin intermediarios, 30-50% bajo precio público. - 📋 Documentación DINAVISA — Incluida con cada embarque. - 📸 Marketing material — Fotos + descripciones en español incluidas.

3. Channels

Direct (DTC): - 📱 Instagram @enkistore (primary, 20K followers) - 💬 WhatsApp Business (primary checkout, 90%+ of sales) - 🌐 paragu-ai subdomain kinky.enki.com.py (catalog) - 📧 Email (Brevo, free tier Month 1+) - 🎵 TikTok @kinky_py (Month 3+, separate from Enki)

Indirect (B2B): - 🤝 In-person visits to PY sex shops (top 10 priority list) - 📞 Phone outreach to CDE / Encarnación shops - 📄 B2B wholesale catalog (PDF + WhatsApp)

Future: - 🛒 MercadoLibre PY (Month 3+, if approved) - 📍 Pickup points in Asunción (Month 6+) - 🏨 Hotel partnerships (Month 6+) - 🌎 Regional wholesale (Year 2+)

4. Customer Relationships

Phase 1 (DTC launch, Month 1–3): - Personal: 1-on-1 WhatsApp with Sarah - Self-service: Subdomain catalog browsing - Community: IG follower engagement - Automation: Brevo email flows (welcome, abandoned cart, post-purchase)

Phase 2 (Growth, Month 4–6): - Loyalty program (early access, referral bonuses) - Email nurture sequences - VIP segment for top customers

Phase 3 (Scale, Month 7+): - Subscription box (recurring relationship) - Community / ambassador program - Self-service ordering (Tienda Nube or custom site) - Account-based B2B relationship management
