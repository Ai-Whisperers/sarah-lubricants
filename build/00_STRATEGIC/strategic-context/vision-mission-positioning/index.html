Vision, Mission, Positioning — Enki Store

Date: June 26, 2026 Purpose: Brand essence + market position (informs all copy, marketing, partnerships) Status: v1 — refined from 3 sessions of strategy work

Brand hierarchy (recap)

ENKI STORE (parent brand, existing)
├── Enki (general lifestyle / e-com)
│   └── Existing products (lingerie, etc.)
├── Kinky (sub-brand, intimate wellness)
│   ├── Lubricantes
│   ├── Estimulantes
│   ├── Suplementos (v2)
│   └── Wellness / self-care

Kinky is a section within Enki, not a separate brand. This is the key strategic decision (per enki-store-assessment.md).

Vision

A Paraguay where every adult can access intimate wellness products of the same quality, discretion, and trust as anywhere in the world.

Not "everyone should buy intimate products" — but "if you choose to, you should have access to international-quality options without shame or compromise."

Mission

Make intimate wellness accessible, safe, and beautiful for Paraguayan adults through curated products, transparent information, and a brand you can trust.

Three pillars: - Curated products — we don't sell everything, we sell what we'd use ourselves - Transparent information — real ingredients, real certifications, real prices - Brand you can trust — DINAVISA-certified, real humans behind it, real customer service

Positioning

One-liner (use everywhere)

Bienestar íntimo, sin complejos.

Three-line positioning (for press, partnerships)

Enki Store + Kinky es la primera distribuidora oficial de Sexitive Argentina en Paraguay. Ofrecemos lubricantes, estimulantes, aceites y velas de masaje certificados por DINAVISA, con empaque discreto y atención personalizada por WhatsApp.

Long positioning (for website, About page)

Paraguay merecía acceso a productos de bienestar íntimo de calidad internacional, con la privacidad y el respeto que un tema tan personal requiere. Sarah Roig, fundadora de Enki Store, vio la oportunidad de traer la marca argentina #1 en intimate wellness — Sexitive — al país, con toda la documentación oficial, empaque genérico, y servicio que un tema así merece.

Target audience

Primary: Sarah's existing @enkistore followers (20K)

  • Demographics: ~70% female, 25-40 years old, Asunción + Gran Asunción
  • Psychographics: Values wellness, open to self-care, appreciates brand stories
  • Risk: Existing audience may not align with intimate wellness (untested assumption)

Secondary: New audience via SEO + paid (Month 2+)

  • Demographics: Women 25-45, Asunción + CDE + Encarnación, searching Google
  • Psychographics: Curious about self-care, open to trying new products
  • Channel: Blog content (SEO) → WA conversion

Tertiary: B2B wholesale buyers (Month 4+)

  • Demographics: PY sex shop owners (5-10 shops)
  • Need: Reliable Sexitive supply at Mayorista pricing
  • Channel: Direct visits + WA relationship

Value proposition (3 levels)

Functional (what we do)

  • Distribute Sexitive Argentina products in Paraguay
  • All products DINAVISA-registered
  • Discrete packaging
  • WA-first ordering

Emotional (how it feels)

  • Trust (DINAVISA paperwork, real humans, transparent pricing)
  • Relief (no more "consultar" or shame — clear pricing + privacy)
  • Discovery (educational content, curated SKUs, real info)

Self-expressive (what it says about you)

  • "I take care of my intimate wellness" (not a taboo)
  • "I value quality" (Sexitive AR, not Chinese generic)
  • "I support a local entrepreneur" (Sarah, Paraguay)

Brand personality

Enki Store (parent)

  • Warm, honest, like a friend giving advice
  • Lifestyle / general e-com tone
  • Existing audience expectations

Kinky (sub-brand)

  • Slightly playful, sensual, but always discreet
  • "Modern, sex-positive, not vulgar"
  • Wellness-first framing (not explicit)

Tone of voice

What we say

  • ✅ "Bienestar íntimo sin complejos"
  • ✅ "Tu privacidad es nuestra prioridad"
  • ✅ "Certificado por DINAVISA"
  • ✅ "Directo de Sexitive Argentina"
  • ✅ "Empaque genérico, nadie sabe qué es"
  • ✅ "Asesoramiento personalizado por WhatsApp"
  • ✅ "Para vos, sin tabúes"

What we DON'T say

  • ❌ "Sex shop" (associated with adult store stigma)
  • ❌ "Hot" / "Passionate" / over-sexualized
  • ❌ "Porn" / "XXX" (algorithmic filters)
  • ❌ Clinical / medical language
  • ❌ "Cheap" / "Affordable" (position as quality, not budget)
  • ❌ Compare to competitors negatively
  • ❌ Religious / political language

For adults in Paraguay who value quality, privacy, and trust in their intimate wellness products, Enki Store + Kinky is the first official distributor of Sexitive Argentina in the country, unlike generic adult shops or anonymous online retailers, we offer curated products with DINAVISA certification, discrete packaging, and personal service via WhatsApp.
