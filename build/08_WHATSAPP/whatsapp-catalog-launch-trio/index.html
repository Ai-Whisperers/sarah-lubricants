How to upload to WA Business

Step-by-step

  1. Open WhatsApp Business app
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon) → Business Tools → Catalog
  3. Tap + Add new item
  4. Fill in: - Images (1-10 photos, max 5MB each) - Name (max 60 chars) - Price (with currency) - Description (max 500 chars) - Link (optional, but use for SEO) - Item code/SKU (for inventory tracking)
  5. Tap Save

Image specs

  • Format: JPG or PNG
  • Recommended: 1024×1024 px (square, white background)
  • Max size: 5MB per image
  • Best: Use Sexitive's official product photos with permission
