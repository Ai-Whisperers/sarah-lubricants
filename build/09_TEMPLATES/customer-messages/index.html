Customer Message Templates

Date: June 26, 2026 Audience: Sarah (operator) — paste-and-send ready Purpose: Standardize customer-facing messages for consistency + professionalism

Use: When customer asks about returns.

✅ Cambio dentro de 7 días si el producto

te lo cambio sin costo. Solo mandame foto 
del problema.

Tone guidelines

Tone When
Warm + casual Welcome, thank-you, follow-up
Professional + brief Order confirm, shipping, payment
Empathetic Complaints, delays, OOS
Educational Product questions, first-time buyers
Brief + direct Repeat customers who know the products

Always: - ✅ Emojis sparingly (1–3 per message) - ✅ Customer's name once or twice - ✅ Clear next step ("¿Confirmamos?") - ✅ No pushy language - ❌ Never medical claims ("vas a tener orgasmos múltiples") - ❌ Never shame the customer ("es muy fácil de usar") - ❌ Never compare negatively to competitors
