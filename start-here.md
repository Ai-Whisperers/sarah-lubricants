## The business in one paragraph

Sarah wants to bring **Sexitive** (an Argentine intimate-wellness brand with 12 SKUs) into Paraguay and sell them. Sexitive is a real brand, headquartered in Martinez, Buenos Aires, that already sells wholesale to resellers across LATAM. The catalog covers four product clusters: stimulants (Bitchie, Pink Sexy Pill, XXX), lubricants (Wet line, more sex), sensory (Love Potion, candles), and wellness (My Hemp CBD).

## The 3 things that have to be true before we build anything

| # | Question | Why it matters | Where it's answered |
|---|---------|----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **What is the legal model?** Authorized distributor, independent reseller, or private label? | Determines brand, contracts, customs declarations, and Sexitive's willingness to supply. | Questionnaire §2 |
| 2 | **What is the cost basis per SKU?** What Sarah actually pays, not retail AR. | Determines if margins work. Without this, pricing is fiction. | Questionnaire §3 |
| 3 | **Which SKUs are we launching with?** Not all 12 may be legal/economic in PY. | Pink Sexy Pill needs supplement registration. My Hemp needs CBD handling. Some may be unprofitable after duties. | Questionnaire §4 |

If §1, §2, §4 are answered, the project is unblocked. Everything else is execution.
