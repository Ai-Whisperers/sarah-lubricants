## Strengths (what they do well)

1. **Multi-category depth** — 10+ categories = broader addressable market
2. **WA-asistoria** — emphasizes "personas con experiencia" (trust signal)
3. **Discrete shipping** — "Pedido Discreto" prominent on site
4. **Tone is friendly + sex-positive** — "olvídate de los juicios"
5. **"Experiencia" differentiator** — frames as expert-driven, not anonymous
6. **Same-day delivery** option (Asunción area)

## Weaknesses (where Sarah can beat them)

1. **Generic products** — they sell Chinese dropship toys, not branded intimate wellness
2. **No educational content** — zero blog, zero SEO play
3. **No active social media visible** (per scrape) — IG presence weak
4. **No premium brand** — no Argentine (Sexitive) or premium (LELO, Dame) brands
5. **No subscription / bundle strategy** — one-off purchases
6. **B2B channel absent** — pure DTC
7. **No transparency on product origins** — "the best providers" but not specific
8. **Anonymity** — no founder story, no voice, no community
