## Why Kinky Store?

**Pros:**
- Direct, memorable, brand-able
- Instagram handle available (@kinkystore_py or similar)
- .com.py domain available (kinkystore.com.py)
- Modern, edgy positioning — appeals to Gen Z + Millennials
- Sex-positive tone matches audience expectations

**Cons:**
- "Kinky" triggers Meta/Google ad filters (organic reach limits)
- Word "kinky" polarized — older audience may reject
- DINAPI requires classes 3 (cosmetics), 5 (lubricants), 35 (retail) all covered
- Younger demographic skew (< 25) may have lower purchasing power
