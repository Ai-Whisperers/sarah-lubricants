## The Pink Sexy Pill path (for when capital allows)

Once Sarah's first 2–3 months generate revenue:

1. Set aside Gs 5–8M from revenue.
2. Engage Sexitive AR manager to get the **Certificado de Libre Venta from ANMAT** (AR equivalent of DINAVISA).
3. Engage a local **despachante** to file the DINAVISA supplement registration.
4. Submit paperwork with ANMAT certificate + formulation + labels.
5. Wait 90–180 days for DINAVISA response.
6. First Pink Sexy Pill shipment arrives 4–6 months into the business.
7. Add to catalog with marketing hook: "The supplement everyone's been waiting for."

**Total additional capital: Gs 5–8M (reg) + Gs 30–50K per unit (stock).**
