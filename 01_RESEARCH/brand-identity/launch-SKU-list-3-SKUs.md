## Why this trio (rationale)

### Why Bitchie 20ml
- 🟢 Best-seller in Sexitive AR (per Sarah)

- 🟡 ASP AR$ 12,200 = Gs 48,800 retail; landed ~Gs 65K/u at 30u order = Gs 130K @ 100% margin

### Why XXX For Her 15ml
- 🟢 Female-focused (matches Enki's female-skewed audience)
- 🟢 Highest-AR-retail in launch set (AR$ 16,540 = Gs 66,160)
- 🟢 Unique positioning (no PY competitor sells this product)
- 🟢 DINAVISA Cosmético NSO
- 🟡 Landed ~Gs 73K/u = Gs 145K @ 100% margin (still in market)

### Why Wet Anal 75ml (Sarah's choice over Like a Virgin)
- 🟢 Sarah's preference (Q2 verbatim)
- 🟡 Commodity (all 5 competitors sell anal lubes) — **volume driver, not differentiator**
- 🟢 Cheapest landed cost (~Gs 63K/u)
- 🟢 DINAVISA Cosmético NSO
- 🔴 **Strategic downgrade vs. Like a Virgin** — flagged June 22
  - Like a Virgin is unique in PY market (no competitor)
  - Wet Anal requires competing on price (Rivia, SexShop.com.py all sell it)
  - Sarah chose volume-over-differentiation. Acceptable if her goal = "validate market fast"
