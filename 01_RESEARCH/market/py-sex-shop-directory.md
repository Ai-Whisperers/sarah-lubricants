## B2B Wholesale Pricing Strategy

| Tier | Discount from AR retail | MOQ | Payment |
|------|-------------------------|-----|---------|
| **Mayorista A** (Tier 1 shops) | 40–50% | 30u/SKU | 50% anticipado, 50% contra entrega |
| **Mayorista B** (Tier 2 shops) | 30–40% | 15u/SKU | 100% anticipado |
| **Lingería shelf** (Tier 3) | 25–35% | 5u/SKU | Revenue share 60/40 |

**Reference:** Sarah's wholesale cost = Distribuidor Mayorista ≈ 50% of AR retail. Selling at 40–50% discount to retailer = ~break-even for Sarah on first sale. Need to upsell or set higher tiers.

**Better pricing:** Sarah should sell B2B at Distribuidor (not Mayorista) cost + 30% markup. That's still 20% below retail for the buyer, with Sarah making ~30% margin.
