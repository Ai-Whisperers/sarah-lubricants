## Where Sexitive has a clear edge in PY

1. **Pink Sexy Pill** — almost no PY competitor carries a libido supplement. If DINAVISA approves, Sarah is alone in this category.
2. **Wet — Like a Virgin** — unique formulation. No PY equivalent. Best flagship candidate.
3. **My Hemp CBD** — first-mover if regulatory clears. Otherwise drop from launch.

## Where Sexitive competes head-to-head

- Standard Wet line (Anal, Ice Fresh) — most PY competitors carry equivalents. Sarah wins on brand, not on product.
- Edible flavored lubes (more sex) — commodity category. Margin pressure.
- Massage candles — differentiator is assortment breadth (12 scents), not product.
- Body oils (Love Potion) — commodity category.
