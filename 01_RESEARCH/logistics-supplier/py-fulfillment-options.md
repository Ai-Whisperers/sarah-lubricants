## Packaging recommendations

### Discrete unbranded outer box
- Plain brown cardboard box (no Sexitive, no Enki, no "intimate" markings)
- Size: 20×15×10 cm for typical single-SKU order
- Cost: Gs 2,000–4,000/box
- MOQ: 100 (local imprentas)

### Internal packaging
- Black tissue paper (premium feel)
- Generic thank-you card (Enki Store + WA contact)
- Optional: small sample sachet (more sex 5ml or similar)
- Cost: Gs 1,000–2,000/order

### Stickers (dual-brand)
- Bilingual sticker overlay (Spanish) on Sexitive product
- Includes: Sarah's RUC, importador info, lote, vencimiento
- Cost: Gs 200–500/sticker
- MOQ: 500 (imprentas Asunción)

## Fulfillment SOP (pack-ship standard)

```
1. Receive WA message with order (Sarah or assistant)
2. Confirm stock in spreadsheet (Google Sheets, shared)
3. Send payment instructions (transfer / Tigo / MP)
4. Receive payment confirmation
5. Pick product from storage
6. Verify SKU + lote + vencimiento
7. Apply bilingual sticker overlay
8. Wrap in tissue paper
9. Place in discrete box with thank-you card
10. Add packing slip (no prices, just product list)
11. Apply shipping label (AEX/Mirtrans)
12. Send customer tracking number via WA
13. Update order status to "shipped"
14. Follow up at Day 3: "¿Te llegó?"
15. At Day 7: request review / UGC
```

**Time per order:** 8–12 minutes (practice → 5 min)
**Throughput:** 1 person = 30–40 orders/day
