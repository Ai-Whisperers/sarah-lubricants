# AR → PY Freight — Options, Costs, Lead Times

**Purpose:** Document the freight and customs clearance options for Sexitive AR → Paraguay.
**Last verified:** June 19, 2026

---

## Three freight modes

### Mode 1 — International courier (DHL, FedEx, UPS, ARCA courier)

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Best for** | First small order (5–20 kg), market testing, samples |
| **Lead time** | 7–15 days door-to-door |
| **Cost** | USD 8–15/kg + customs clearance fee |
| **Pros** | Fast, simple, door-to-door, low minimums |
| **Cons** | Expensive per kg, may not be discreet, courier customs handling is rough |
| **Recommended for** | First 1–3 orders while DINAVISA registration is in progress |

### Mode 2 — Consolidated freight forwarder (Maritima / courier consolidado)

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Best for** | Regular orders 50–500 kg |
| **Lead time** | 15–30 days end-to-end (BUENOS AIRES → ASUNCIÓN) |
| **Cost** | USD 4–8/kg + clearance + storage |
| **Pros** | Cheaper per kg, more flexible, can negotiate discreet handling |
| **Cons** | Slower, requires more coordination, warehouse consolidation |
| **Recommended for** | Ongoing supply, post-Model-1 transition |

### Mode 3 — Air freight forwarder (Cargolux, LAN Cargo, etc.)

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Best for** | Time-sensitive orders 100+ kg |
| **Lead time** | 5–10 days |
| **Cost** | USD 6–12/kg + clearance |
| **Pros** | Faster than consolidated, can move pallet quantities |
| **Cons** | Still needs customs broker, not door-to-door |
| **Recommended for** | Restocking hot SKUs, promotions |

## Customs broker (Despachante) — what to look for

| Quality | What it looks like |
|---------|-------------------|
| Registered with DNA | Verify at www.aduana.gov.py |
| Experience with cosmetics / DINAVISA | Ask specifically |
| Bilingual (Spanish + English) | Helps if Sexitive is English-speaking |
| Responsive on WhatsApp | Critical for time-sensitive shipments |
| Transparent fee structure | Should quote despachante fee upfront |

**A good despachante is the difference between a 15-day clearance and a 60-day one. Don't pick the cheapest; pick the most reliable.**

## Discreet packaging (intimate products)

| Option | Pros | Cons |
|--------|------|------|
| **Sexitive ships in unmarked outer carton** | No one at courier knows contents | Sexitive may charge extra or not offer |
| **Sarah re-packages in plain cartons at AR consolidation warehouse** | Full control | Adds labor cost, requires warehouse access |
| **Custom discreet outer packaging from day 1** | Best long-term | Higher upfront design cost |
| **No special handling, rely on shipping label** | Simplest | Risk of neighbor/courier seeing "Sexitive" on label |

**Recommended:** Negotiate with Sexitive AR for unmarked outer cartons for first 3 shipments. If they can do it, great. If not, plan a re-pack step at the AR consolidation warehouse.

**Critical for the customer:** The product arrives in a plain carton with no external marking indicating "Sexitive" or "intimate products." Inner packaging is the Sexitive retail box (which is itself discreetly designed).
