- **Conversion path:** Product share in chat → order summary → payment

## Tier 2 — ACTIVATE BY MONTH 2–3

### 5. Custom e-com site (enkystore.com.py) 🟡
- **Type:** Full e-commerce with cart + checkout
- **Platform:** Next.js custom (clone fun4me-store pattern) OR Shopify Lite
- **Activation effort:** 3–4 weeks + Gs 50K–200K domain
- **Revenue potential:** High long-term (own audience, SEO, brand)
- **Risk:** Payment integration in PY is non-trivial
- **Decision needed:** Platform choice (see `01_RESEARCH/operations/ecom-platform-decision.md`)

### 6. Email list (Mailchimp / Brevo) 🟡
- **Type:** Newsletter, new arrivals, exclusive offers, education
- **Conversion path:** IG/WA → email opt-in → nurture → sale
- **Activation effort:** Medium — needs lead magnet (educational PDF, 10% off first order)
- **Revenue potential:** 🔴 HIGHEST CONVERSION per touch (3–5x social)
- **Cost:** Free up to 1,000 subscribers (Mailchimp Free tier / Brevo Free)

### 7. TikTok PY account (new) 🟡
- **Type:** Short videos, lives, educational content
- **Activation effort:** Medium — needs content production
- **Revenue potential:** 🔴 HIGH — TikTok is the most permissive intimate-content platform
- **Risk:** Meta may shadowban intimate content; TikTok less so
- **Decision:** Build separate @kinky_py or use @enkistore? Recommend separate for ad-policy reasons

### 8. Seasonal campaigns (Valentines, Xmas, Mother's Day) 🟡
- **Type:** IG/email/WA blasts + bundles
- **Activation effort:** Low (just campaign)
- **Revenue potential:** 🔴 HIGH — 20–30% of annual revenue in 2 weeks (Valentines)
- **Calendar:** Valentine's Day (Feb 14), Mother's Day (May 15 in PY), Christmas (Dec 25), Day of Love (Sep 14 PY)

### 9. MercadoLibre PY 🟡
- **Type:** Marketplace listing
- **Pros:** Built-in traffic, ML logistics, PY consumer trust
- **Cons:** ML has restrictions on adult content; commission 13–16%; careful listing needed
- **Activation effort:** Medium
- **Revenue potential:** High volume if approved
- **Test plan:** List 1 SKU (Bitchie) first, monitor 30 days, scale if approved
