## Headline finding

**PY intimate-wellness search volume is LOW and SEASONAL:**
- Peak: **February** (Valentine's Day + Day of Love Feb 14)
- Secondary peak: **December** (Christmas gifting)
- Tertiary: **September** (Day of Love Sep 14, Mother's Day May 15)
- Low: **January, June–August** (no demand driver)

**Implication:** Content calendar should front-load Q4 + Feb. Inventory build-up should target Nov–Jan for Feb peak.
