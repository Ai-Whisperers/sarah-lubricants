## What to NOT do

- ❌ Don't post nudity (IG shadowban risk)
- ❌ Don't use words "sex", "sexual" in hashtags (filter triggers)
- ❌ Don't fake testimonials (legal + brand risk)
- ❌ Don't copy competitor posts verbatim (legal + boring)
- ❌ Don't post more than 2x/day (engagement drop)
- ❌ Don't use banned hashtags: #sex #porn #nsfw (shadowban)
