> Totalmente normal. Muchos clientes empiezan probando solos. El packaging es super discreto — caja genérica, sin marcas, sin logos. Nadie sabe qué es.

#### "No tengo tarjeta / solo efectivo"

> Perfecto, podés pagar por transferencia bancaria (Bancard, Itaú, Continental) o Tigo Money. Te paso los datos cuando confirmes el pedido.

#### "Cuánto tarda en llegar?"

#### "Puedo pasar a retirar?"

## Pricing rules

| Rule | Reason |
|------|--------|
| **Always show prices upfront** | Transparency = trust. Competitors hide behind "consultar" |
| **Round numbers** | Gs 130,000 not Gs 129,500. Easier mental math |
| **Promo discounts only on bundles** | Single-SKU sales = full price. 2-packs = 10–15% off |
| **Free shipping Asunción** | Removes a friction point |
| **Interior shipping flat** | Gs 25–50K flat, regardless of weight (within reason) |
| **No haggling above 5%** | Margin too tight. "El precio es el precio." |

## What NOT to do

- ❌ Don't be salesy. Be helpful, recommend based on need
- ❌ Don't promise results ("vas a tener 5 orgasmos") — regulatory risk
- ❌ Don't share other customers' info (privacy)
- ❌ Don't sell to minors (legal + moral)
- ❌ Don't ship without payment confirmation
- ❌ Don't promise delivery date you can't meet
- ❌ Don't ship without discrete packaging
- ❌ Don't use WhatsApp status to advertise (use Instagram for that)
